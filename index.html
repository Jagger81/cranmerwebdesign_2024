Mobile First | Responsive Design.

At Cranmer Web Design we practice a strict Mobile First design policy; latest statistical reports estimate the smartphones and other small mobile devices now account for as much as 75% of all internet traffic. Mobile traffice now dominates online business and so it is crucial that your website looks and feels good for mobile audiences and clients.

But we also ensure that all of our templates and designs are responsive, meaning they will adapt in appearance depending on the device the website is being viewed on. As there is a diverse range of screens that people are now using, whether it be a smartphone, a tablet, a laptop or even a larger TV screen; we'll ensure your website will looks great on all screen sizes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new website cost?

Our prices can vary depending on our clients needs (e.g. is a Logo required, bespoke imagery or layouts). But as an estimate, our average price for full website including Domain Regristration and Hosting is €350.

An annual domain fee of approx €45 will also need to be paid to the domain regsistrar.

Can I set up an Online Store?

Unfortunately, this a service which we currently do not offer, as it requires a back-developer. Secure online payment mechanisms or booking systems require an additional piece of work to the front-end design.

However, we can offer you advice or assistance on using platforms such as Ecwid, Wix, Shopify, etc. as these platforms provide this functionality and still allow us to work on the Website Design for you - we'd suggest getting in touch for more info on this.

Do I need to have a Domain Registered already?

No, we can take care of this for you. Currently we use Hosting Ireland to register and set-up all our client domains.

We ensure everything is configured properly and all you will need to do is pay the annual subscription fee of approx. €45.



Do I need to pay more for Hosting?

No, all of our Websites are hosted securely free of charge, so you never to pay extra for this service.

Will I be able to edit the site myself?

Unfortunately not. The code is store and hosting on our side and therefore any amendments would need to be performed by us - however, we have no problem making subtle changes and we are very responsive to such requests.

Can I get a copy of the logo?

Yes, of course! We can send you transparent PNG version, in different formats so you can use them on business cards / stationery.
